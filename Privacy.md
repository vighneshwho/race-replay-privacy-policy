# Privacy Policy for Box-Replay

## Overview
Box-Replay respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

## Data Collection
Box-Replay does **not collect or store any personal data** from within the app or screensaver.

## Purchase Information
When you purchase Box-Replay, your email address is collected by our payment provider (Gumroad) to process the transaction.  

- We do not collect this information directly.
- We do not store or process any additional personal data beyond what is required for the purchase.

## Data Usage
We do not use your data for:
- Tracking
- Analytics
- Advertising

## App Behavior
The Box-Replay screensaver:
- Does not track user activity  
- Does not transmit any data  
- Does not store any user information locally or remotely  

## Third-Party Services
Purchases are handled by third-party platforms (Gumroad). Their respective privacy policies govern how your purchase data is handled.
